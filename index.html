The Moroccan AngularJS Developer Community

The main goal of ngMorocco is to share AngularJS knowledge and best-practices, to gather people who are passionate about AngularJS and enable them to talk, socialize and learn from the best, with delicious food and a friendly atmosphere no matter your gender, age, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion.
